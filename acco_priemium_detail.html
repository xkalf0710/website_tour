Tsagaan Suvarga Tourist Camp

Overview Rooms Food Facilities Promotional video

Overview

The white stupa camp offers a unique retreat next to the limestone cliffs. It's easy for visitors to enjoy stunning views, cultural experiences, and natural wonders of Mongolia such as the Baga Land Stone.

Address

From Ulaanbaatar, drive 430 km south to Mandalgovi, then 42 km east on dirt roads to Tsagaan Suvarga camp, following signs for this stunning limestone destination.
