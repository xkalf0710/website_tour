Overview

Embark on a 12-day expedition into the heart of Southern Mongolia with the Singing Southern Dunes tour. This immersive experience brings together cultural landmarks, breathtaking natural wonders, and the unique thrill of camel riding. You’ll explore the awe-inspiring Khongoryn Els sand dunes and the legendary Flaming Cliffs, promising an unforgettable journey through the Gobi Desert.

Pricing & Inclusions

We know that every traveler is unique. That’s why we offer flexible pricing options to suit your style and budget—each designed to let you explore Mongolia your way.

Adventurer Package | $70–$100 per day

Perfect for the independent traveler, this option is priced per day so you can decide when to go, where to stop, and how long to linger. Enjoy complete freedom on the road while consulting with our experts on your itinerary. For those who crave a true camping adventure, we also offer tent and sleeping bag rentals, adjusting the rate to the higher end of the range. Choose your vehicle—whether it’s the rugged Uaz Purgon, a versatile 4-wheeled drive like a Landcruiser, or a Starex—and set off on an adventure that’s truly yours.

Budget Explorer Package | $110–$160 per day

Ideal for travelers seeking a well-curated journey on a budget, this package follows a static, thoughtfully designed itinerary covering all the essentials. It includes your driver, fuel, vehicle, entry tickets, and budget-friendly accommodations that ensure a comfortable experience without overspending. Every detail is planned to keep your Mongolian tour affordable while still capturing the magic of the land.

Signature Experience | $180–$230 per day

Our top recommendation for those who want the complete, worry-free experience. This package adds a professional guide to the mix, along with a driver, fuel, vehicle, entry tickets, and accommodations at a recommended service level. Enjoy a balanced journey that combines expert guidance with exceptional service—ensuring every moment of your Mongolian adventure is memorable.

No matter which package you choose, our fleet options—ranging from Uaz Purgon to Landcruisers and Starex—ensure we can match the perfect vehicle to your tour and budget. And remember, anything is possible! With open communication, we can tailor the itinerary and pricing to create the exact tour you envision.
