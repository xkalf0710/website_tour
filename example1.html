Day 1 — Genghis Khan Statue Complex & Terelj National Park

Start your journey at the towering Genghis Khan Statue Complex, where you can enjoy panoramic views and learn about Mongolia’s history. Then, head to Terelj National Park to explore iconic landmarks such as Turtle Rock and the serene Aryapal Monastery.

📍 Travel Distance: ~100 KM

🕒 Driving Time: ~3 Hours 30 Minutes

🎯 Activities: Explore Turtle Rock and Aryapal Monastery

🍽️ Meals:

  • Breakfast – Ulaanbaatar
  • Lunch – Genghis Khan Statue Complex
  • Dinner – Terelj National Park (Overnight in ger camp)
