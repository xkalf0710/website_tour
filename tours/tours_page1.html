KharaKhorum Tour | 07 Days 06 Nights | May 23 – May 30, 2024

Overview

Embark on a 12-day expedition into the heart of Southern Mongolia with the Singing Southern Dunes tour. This immersive experience brings together cultural landmarks, breathtaking natural wonders, and the unique thrill of camel riding. You’ll explore the awe-inspiring Khongoryn Els sand dunes and the legendary Flaming Cliffs, promising an unforgettable journey through the Gobi Desert.

Tour itinerary

Day 1 Genghis Khan Statue Complex & Terelj National Park

Overview

 

Journey to Ugii Lake, a serene destination perfect for birdwatching, boating, or simply relaxing by the water. Capture the tranquility of the lake through photography or take part in optional fishing activities.

Travel Distance: ~100 KM

Driving Time: ~3 Hours 30 Minutes

Activities: Explore Turtle Rock and Aryapal Monastery

Meals:

  • Breakfast ~ Ulaanbaatar
  • Lunch ~ Genghis Khan Statue Complex
  • Dinner ~ Terelj National Park Overnight in ger camp
  
Day 2 Khustai Nuruu National Park

Overview

 

Spend the day at Khustai Nuruu National Park, where you'll spot the famous Przewalski's horses and experience the park's rich wildlife and beautiful landscapes.

Travel Distance: ~100 KM

Driving Time: ~3 Hours 30 Minutes

Activities: Spot Przewalski's horses in their natural habitat

Meals:

  • Breakfast ~ Ulaanbaatar
  • Lunch ~ Genghis Khan Statue Complex
  • Dinner ~ Terelj National Park Camp rear the park
  
Day 3 Ugii Lake

Overview

 

Journey to Ugii Lake, a serene destination perfect for birdwatching, boating, or simply relaxing by the water. Capture the tranquility of the lake through photography or take part in optional fishing activities.

Travel Distance: ~100 KM

Driving Time: ~3 Hours 30 Minutes

Activities: Trek to Altai Tavan Bogd Base Camp Explore wildlife en route to Altai Tavan Bogd Local Market Exploration

Day 4 Erdenezuu Monastery & Kharkhorin

Overview

 

Visit the ancient Erdenezuu Monastery, a cornerstone of Mongolian Buddhism. Spend the evening exploring Kharkhorin, the former capital of the Mongol Empire, steeped in history and culture.

Travel Distance: ~100 KM

Driving Time: ~3 Hours 30 Minutes

Activities: Explore Erdenezuu Monastery & Walk through the historic town of Kharkhorin

Meals:

  • Breakfast ~ Ugii Lake
  • Lunch ~ Erdenezuu Monastery
  • Dinner ~ Kharkhorin Overnight in ger camp
  
Day 5 Nomadic Family & Elsen Tasarkhai

Overview

Spend the morning with a nomadic family, learning about their traditions and daily life. Then, head to Elsen Tasarkhai for a camel ride amidst its stunning sand dunes.

Travel Distance: ~100 KM

Driving Time: ~3 Hours 30 Minutes

Activities: Explore Turtle Rock and Aryapal Monastery Ride camels across the Elsen Tasarkhai dunes

Meals:

  • Breakfast ~ Kharkhorin
  • Lunch ~ Nomadic Family
  • Dinner ~ Elsen Tasarkhai Overnight in ger camp
  
Day 6 Return to Ulaanbaatar

Overview

Conclude your adventure with a scenic drive back to Ulaanbaatar, making optional stops for souvenirs or sightseeing before wrapping up your journey.

PRICING OVERVIEW

We know that every traveler is unique. That’s why we offer flexible pricing options to suit your style and budget—each designed to let you explore Mongolia your way.

Adventurer Package | $70–$100 per day

Perfect for the independent traveler, this option is priced per day so you can decide when to go, where to stop, and how long to linger. Enjoy complete freedom on the road while consulting with our experts on your itinerary. For those who crave a true camping adventure, we also offer tent and sleeping bag rentals, adjusting the rate to the higher end of the range. Choose your vehicle—whether it’s the rugged Uaz Purgon, a versatile 4-wheeled drive like a Landcruiser, or a Starex—and set off on an adventure that’s truly yours.

Budget Explorer Package | $110–$160 per day

Ideal for travelers seeking a well-curated journey on a budget, this package follows a static, thoughtfully designed itinerary covering all the essentials. It includes your driver, fuel, vehicle, entry tickets, and budget-friendly accommodations that ensure a comfortable experience without overspending. Every detail is planned to keep your Mongolian tour affordable while still capturing the magic of the land.

Signature Experience | $180–$230 per day

Our top recommendation for those who want the complete, worry-free experience. This package adds a professional guide to the mix, along with a driver, fuel, vehicle, entry tickets, and accommodations at a recommended service level. Enjoy a balanced journey that combines expert guidance with exceptional service—ensuring every moment of your Mongolian adventure is memorable.

No matter which package you choose, our fleet options—ranging from Uaz Purgon to Landcruisers and Starex—ensure we can match the perfect vehicle to your tour and budget. And remember, anything is possible! With open communication, we can fine-tune the itinerary and pricing to create the exact tour you envision.
